\chapter*{Appendix: Model Selection History}
\renewcommand{\thetable}{A.\arabic{table}}
\setcounter{table}{0}
This appendix consolidates every configuration explored during model
selection, so that any choice made in the body of the thesis can be traced
to the attempt that motivated it. Two records are given. Table~\ref{tab:appfamily}
is the detector-family benchmark that fixed the YOLO11m baseline; it was run
under an earlier protocol (different hardware and schedule), so its absolute
values are not comparable to the final ablation of Table~\ref{tab:main} and
it serves only to justify the backbone choice; $P$, $R$, and $F_1$ follow
the definitions of Section~\ref{sec:metrics}, and Size is the weight-file
size. Table~\ref{tab:apphistory}
then lists every architectural variant attempted across the project with its
outcome and the decision it produced, including the attempts that failed and
the one whose first results were invalidated by an implementation fault.

    \midrule

    CBAM attention & C2PSA $\to$ CBAM in backbone & $F_2$ 0.849, small-object recall 0.892 (earlier protocol); confirmed in final chain (Table~\ref{tab:main}) & adopted \\
    P2 detection scale & adds stride-4 head branch & $\mathrm{AP}_{50}$ $0.843 \to 0.853$, very-tiny recall $0.743 \to 0.757$ (final protocol) & adopted; core of recommended model \\
    C3k2Mamba, first attempt & post-initialisation SSM injection into neck & results invalid: the framework's training rebuild silently removed the injected blocks, so the model trained was CBAM~+~P2 & discarded; protocol gained module-presence verification \\
    AtrousSSM & multi-scale dilated SSM scanning in neck & prototype stage; smoke evaluations showed no reliable gain and no protocol-compliant full run was completed & set aside \\
    C3k2Mamba, corrected & verified SSM blocks at six neck layers & genuine null: $+2.4$~M parameters, $2.8\times$ latency, no accuracy change (Table~\ref{tab:main}); scan-activity diagnostics confirm the blocks ran & excluded from recommendation \\
