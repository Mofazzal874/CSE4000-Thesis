Main Points: 
- 1. Hybrid detection pipelines
Use strong object detectors like YOLO or DETR but enhance them with:

Mamba-based context features to model temporal or spatial dependencies across frames,

Diffusion priors for noise and occlusion resilience,

Sequence modelling for aerial surveillance video streams rather than static images.

This targets situational awareness in motion rather than static detection.

2. Change and motion focus
Instead of just classifying humans frame by frame:

Use Mamba and diffusion frameworks for change motion patterns over time to detect human movement,

Combine temporal context with detection scores to reduce false positives in disaster debris.

This moves beyond plain bounding boxes into behavioral or context inference.
