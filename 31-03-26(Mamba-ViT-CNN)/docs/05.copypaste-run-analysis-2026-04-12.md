## WHAT WENT WRONG

### 1. Medium object recall collapsed: 0.896 -> 0.763 (-13.3%)
This is the single biggest red flag. Copy-paste at 0.5 probability was too aggressive — it flooded training images with pasted small objects, causing the model to over-focus on tiny detections at the expense of medium-sized ones. The model essentially got "distracted" by all the artificially pasted small objects.

### 2. Very-tiny gain is negligible: +0.3%
The whole point of copy-paste was to boost very-tiny recall. A +0.3% gain (0.7668 -> 0.7698) is within noise margin. This is NOT a meaningful improvement.

### 3. Overall metrics all degraded
mAP50-95 dropped by 1.67 points. F1 dropped. F2 dropped. Calibration got worse (ECE 0.134 -> 0.146). The model is less accurate AND less well-calibrated.

### 4. The augmentation was likely too aggressive
The config used: `copy_paste=0.5, mixup=0.15, flipud=0.5, degrees=10.0`. Four augmentations stacked together. The model couldn't learn clean representations because every training image was heavily modified.

## WHAT WENT RIGHT

- All 120 epochs completed (total wall time: ~29,423 seconds = ~8.2 hours)
- No NaN/Inf loss events
- No OOM crashes
- Best epoch: 110 (model kept improving until late)
- Loss curves show smooth convergence with no oscillation
- The close_mosaic=10 transition at epoch 110 is visible in the loss drop

### 2. SAHI evaluation worked and shows real promise
Best SAHI config: **512x512 slices, 0.25 overlap**

| Metric | Standard | + SAHI (512) | Delta |
|--------|----------|-------------|-------|
| Precision | 0.8496 | 0.8812 | +0.0316 |

SAHI gives a **+5.0% boost to very-tiny recall** — this is the real win, not copy-paste. Latency increases to 190.8ms (acceptable for SAR applications, not real-time).

### 3. TTA evaluation shows significant mAP50-95 gains

| Config | mAP50 | mAP50-95 |

| No TTA (640) | 0.8690 | 0.6276 |

TTA at 1024 gives **+1.6% mAP50** and **+4.7% mAP50-95**. This is a substantial inference-time improvement with zero retraining.

### 4. Training curves are textbook
- Precision plateaued at ~0.876 (healthy, not overfitting)

### 5. Confusion matrix looks correct
